    jumeg.connectivity.get_label_distances¶

    jumeg.connectivity.get_label_distances(subject, subjects_dir, parc='aparc')¶

    Get the Eucliden distance between label center of mass and return the distance matrix. The distance are computed between vertices in the MNI coordinates in the subject source space.

    Parameters subject: str

    Name of the subject.

    subjects_dir: str

    The subjects directory.

    parc: str

    Name of the parcellation. Default ‘aparc’.

    Return rounded_com: ndarray | (N, N)

    The distance between center of masses of different labels

    coords_all: ndarray | (N, )

    The MNI coordinates of the vertices in the source space.

    coms_lh, coms_rh: list | (N, )

    The centre of masses of labels in left and right hemispheres.
